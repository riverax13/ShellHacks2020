How Can I Help?

The California Fires has burned over 3.6 million acres in the state of California alone and surrounding states can feel the after affects created by this devestation. With over 17,400 Firefighters fighting in the front lines and over 20,000 people affected, Pickmen & Co. have compiled resources to help those that are currently fighting or are being affected by the fires.

The American Red Cross needs your help!

The American Red Cross is in need of volunteers that can help assist those that have been affected. For more information, call 800-RED-CROSS or text REDCROSS to 90999 to make a $10 donation.


California Volunteers needs you!

If you are interested in volunteering, the California Volunteers are seeking volunteers to assist with recovery efforts for the Camp Fire. They are also seeking donations and help for temporary housing.
